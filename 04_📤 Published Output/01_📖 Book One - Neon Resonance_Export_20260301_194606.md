The acid rain hammered against the reinforced glass of Jax's apartment. He stared down at the sprawling mess of Sector 4, watching the hover-trains weave through the glowing skyscrapers owned by OmniCorp. He checked his Neural-Link; the encrypted message from Elara was still there. The job was tonight.

Elara was waiting in the shadows of the rusted alley. When Jax arrived, she tossed him a small, metallic cylinder. "This is the prototype Quantum Drive," she whispered, her eyes darting around the empty street. "If OmniCorp finds out we have this, we're both dead."

"They know we're here!" Jax's voice crackled through the comms. Elara cursed and armed her plasma rifle. "Get to the extraction point at Sector 4! I'll hold them off!" Red alarms painted the corridor in a chaotic strobe light. The heist had just turned into a fight for survival.

Bypassing the main security grid of the OmniCorp tower took less than three minutes with Elara's custom decryption algorithm. Jax slipped through the ventilation shaft, his cybernetic enhancements making his movements completely silent. He dropped into the server room. The glowing blue pillars of data hummed with immense power.
